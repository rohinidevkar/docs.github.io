Block the Cards

Why Block the Card ?

When a Digital card is lost, it needs to be blocked to prevent unauthorized use of the card by someone else. Blocking the card will prevent anyone from using it for fraudulent purposes such as making unauthorized transactions or accessing sensitive information. It is important to block your identity card as soon as possible after you realize it is lost or stolen. If you have lost your identity card, you should contact your bank or the relevant authority immediately to report the loss and request that your card be blocked.
